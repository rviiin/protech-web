Research-Based Innovation

ProTech represents a groundbreaking approach to cybersecurity education, combining game-based learning with advanced Finite State Machines (FSM) AI algorithms to create an immersive educational experience.

In an era where cybersecurity threats continue to evolve, traditional educational methods often fall short of preparing individuals for real-world digital challenges. Our research demonstrates that incorporating interactive gaming elements with structured AI behavior creates a more engaging and effective learning environment.

Through comprehensive testing with third-year students over an entire semester, we measured improvements in interest levels, information retention, and critical thinking skills. The results, validated by IT professionals, show significant enhancement in cybersecurity knowledge acquisition and practical application abilities.

The integration of FSM AI algorithms ensures that in-game characters behave in structured, realistic ways, creating authentic scenarios that mirror actual cybersecurity challenges professionals face daily.
